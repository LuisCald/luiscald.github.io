# BASEforHANK

**Bayesian Solution and Estimation (BASE) for HANK toolbox** is a Julia toolbox for estimating medium-scale HANK (Heterogeneous Agent New Keynesian) models, based on *“Shocks, Frictions, and Inequality in US Business Cycles”* by Christian Bayer, Benjamin Born, and Ralph Luetticke.

- GitHub: https://github.com/BASEforHANK/BASEtoolbox.jl
- Documentation: https://baseforhank.github.io/BASEtoolbox.jl/
